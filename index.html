Biography

I am currently an Associate Researcher at Guangming Laboratory. I received my Ph.D. degree in Optical Engineering from Changchun Institute of Optics, Fine Mechanics and Physics (CIOMP), Chinese Academy of Sciences in 2018. After that I was a engineer of CAST and then did a postdoc research working with Prof. Hui Huang. My research interest generally lies in computer vision and graphics, especially in the area of urban scene understanding.
