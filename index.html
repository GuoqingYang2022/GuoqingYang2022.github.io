Biography

I am currently an Assistant Professor at College of Computer Science & Software Engineering, Shenzhen University (SZU). I received my Ph.D. degree in Photogrammetry and Remote Sensing from Wuhan University in 2013. After that I did a postdoc research working with Prof. Hui Huang, who helped me start my research career in computer graphics. Before joining SZU, I was a software engineer of DJI Innovation and then an assistant professor of Huazhong University of Science and Technology (HUST). My research interest generally lies in computer graphics, including texture synthesis, shape analysis and 3D modeling. Besides graphics, I'm also interested in computer vision and deep learning techniques, especially the deep generative models. I have several publication records both in the top conferences and journals of graphics and vision, including SIGGRAPH, SIGGRAPH Asia, Eurographics, CVPR and NeurIPS.
